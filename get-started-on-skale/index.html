Let’s start with the basics

SCALE's BUILT DIFFERENT

SCALE is fundamentally BUILT DIFFERENT. It’s all about making blockchain accessible and user-friendly. SCALE Network is designed to be gas-free and fast, offering a truly invisible blockchain experience. Forget about high fees and complicated blockchain processes, SCALE's unique architecture ensures smooth, hassle-free interactions for both users and developers.

With millions of active users and billions of dollars saved in gas fees, SCALE is pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the world of Web3. Built for scalability, efficiency, and ease of use, SCALE allows anyone to explore decentralized applications (dApps), from gaming to AI to DeFi and social platforms.

Hub Chains vs. App Chains

On SCALE, there are two types of chains that cater to different needs: Hub Chains and App Chains.

Hub Chains

Hub Chains are designed to host multiple dApps on a single blockchain, each dedicated to a specific function within the SCALE ecosystem. Hub Chains offer a more accessible entry point for developers who want to avoid the complexities and costs associated with running a dedicated App Chain.

Calypso Hub

A versatile hub supporting a range of Web3 dApps across various sectors

Europa DeFi Hub

Tailored for DeFi dApps, offering shared computing resources to streamline decentralized finance operations.

Nebula Gaming Hub

Designed for gaming dApps, providing a robust environment for high-performance gaming experiences.

Titan AI Hub

Focused on AI dApps, providing an optimized blockchain environment for machine learning.

App Chains

App Chains are dedicated, customizable blockchains for individual dApps. From gaming to AI to DeFi and Social platforms, these chains are wholely owned by a single dApp. Developers can spin up app chains that are fully customizable according to the unique requirements of their dApps.

Adding sFuel to Your Wallet

SCALE provides a gas-free experience, enabling users to transact seamlessly without the burden of escalating transaction fees.

SCALE utilizes sFuel, a valueless "gas" token that powers all SCALE blockchains and allows them to operate without traditional transaction fees. sFUEL also plays a crucial security role. By rate-limiting transactions through sFUEL, SCALE effectively eliminated the risk of potential DDoS attacks and spam transactions.

FAQs

How is SCALE gas-free?

SCALE operates with a unique model where developers pay a flat chain subscription fee to host their dApp on a SCALE Hub Chain or to spin up their own SCALE chain. These fees effectively subsidize gas fees for end-users, allowing for a seamless, gas-free transaction experience.

What wallets can I use on SCALE?

The wallets that have confirmed functionality with SCALE’s multi-chain architecture, and therefore are recommended, are Encrypt Wallet, Metamask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, and Rabby Wallet.

Can I bridge assets from any blockchain to SCALE?

Yes, you can bridge assets from over 45+ chains to SCALE using Meson.fi directly via the "Transfer" page on the SCALE Portal.

What is SKL and where is it available?

SKL is the governance token of the SCALE Network. It is an ERC20 token used to reward validators and delegators who stake their SKL tokens to secure the network, as a payment method for developer chain subscription fees, and for on-chain governance. Note that SKL is not a gas token, as SCALE is a gas-free network. You can find the trading markets for SKL via CoinMarketCap or CoinGecko.

What should I do if I face issues while bridging?

If you encounter any issues while bridging, visit the Portal page and click on the "?" button at the top right of the page. Then select "Open Support Chat," explain your issue to the chatbot, and if unresolved, you can open a support ticket. A member of the SCALE team will then get in touch to assist you.

I bridged a token to a SCALE Chain, but I don't see it in my wallet. What should I do?

If you bridged tokens to a SCALE Chain but don't see them in your Metamask, you may need to manually import them as they are not visible by default. To do this first make sure the SCALE Chain is added to your wallet by reviewing the “Adding SCALE Chains to Your Wallet” section above. Then, import the token to your wallet by following step 8 (Add Token to Wallet) from the “Bridging to SCALE” section.

What is sFUEL?

sFUEL is a valueless "gas" token that powers the SCALE blockchains and allows them to operate without traditional transaction costs. sFUEL also plays a crucial security role. By rate-limiting transactions through sFUEL, SCALE effectively curtails the risk of potential DDoS attacks and spam transactions. Without such a mechanism, attackers could flood the network with fake transactions, disrupting service for legitimate users.

How do I stake SKL?
